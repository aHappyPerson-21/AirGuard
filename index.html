Indoor Air Quality Matters

My PYP Exhibition is about indoor air quality and how it affects people every day. Many people stay indoors for a long time, so clean air is important for health, focus, sleep, and learning.

Explore Research Take Air Scan

Why Indoor Air Quality Is Important

Bad indoor air can make people feel tired or sick. It can also affect students in classrooms and people at work.

Respiratory Health

Polluted indoor air can cause coughing, asthma, allergies, and breathing problems.

Brain Performance

High carbon dioxide levels may reduce concentration, memory, and focus in school or offices.

Daily Exposure

People spend many hours indoors at home, school, malls, and workplaces every day.

Environmental Awareness

Learning about indoor air quality can help people build healthier habits and cleaner spaces.

Research Information

These are some important things I learned while researching indoor air pollution and healthy buildings.

Main Sources of Indoor Pollution

Indoor pollution can come from smoke, dust, mold, cleaning products, cooking gases, pets, and poor ventilation.

Sometimes indoor air can actually be more polluted than outdoor air because pollutants stay trapped inside buildings.

  • ✔ Poor ventilation increases carbon dioxide levels.
  • ✔ Mold grows faster in humid places.
  • ✔ Dust can trigger allergies.
  • ✔ Smoke damages lung health.
  • ✔ Air purifiers may help improve air quality.
